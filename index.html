About me

Ritik D is a standup comedian from Haryana, currently working in Chandigarh and Delhi circuit. Starting out at an early age, he has been doing comedy for a year now, winning many college as well as open mic competitions. You can find him on stage often picking out on relatable topics and presenting absurd views on day to day activities. Ritik has been involved in comedy not only as a performer but also as a show host, he successfully hosted his weekly open mic series “Sour balls” between July-September 2018 in Haryana.
